Universal Studios


         

Universal Studiosis a well-known American film studio and entertainment company that is a subsidiary of Comcast. Universal Studios is known for its contributions to the entertainment industry and its ability to create immersive experiences through its theme parks. The Universal theme parks, in particular, are major tourist attractions that draw millions of visitors annually. Universal Studios has a rich history and has been responsible for producing many beloved films and franchises that have had a significant impact on popular culture.
