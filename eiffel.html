Eiffel Tower
Paris, France



The Eiffel Tower is a famous wrought-iron landmark located in Paris, France. It is one of the most recognizable and iconic structures in the world. Gustave Eiffel, a French engineer, designed and oversaw the construction of the tower, which was completed in 1889 and named after him. The Tower stands at a height of approximately 324 meters (1,063 feet) and was the tallest man-made structure in the world at the time of its completion. It consists of three levels that are open to the public, with various viewing platforms offering breathtaking views of Paris.
