Disney World

-DISNEY WORLD-

Orlando, FL, USA


Disney World, officially known as the "Walt Disney World Resort", is a vast entertainment complex located in Lake Buena Vista, Florida, near Orlando. It is one of the most popular and renowned theme parks in the world, attracting millions of visitors annually.

The Walt Disney World Resort was opened on October 1, 1971, and was founded by Walt Disney and Roy O. Disney. It was designed to expand on the success of Disneyland in California and provide a larger and more immersive experience for visitors.
